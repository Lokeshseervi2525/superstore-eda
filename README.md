# 🛒 Superstore Sales - Exploratory Data Analysis

## 📌 Problem Statement
A retail superstore is generating millions in sales but bleeding money in certain regions and product categories. This analysis digs into 9,994 transactions to find out exactly where, why, and how to fix it.

## 🔍 Key Questions Answered
- Which categories generate high sales but low profit?
- At what discount percentage does profit turn negative?
- Which regions are underperforming despite high sales?
- Which sub-categories are loss-makers — and which are hidden timebombs?

## 💡 Key Findings
- **20% discount is the danger threshold** — beyond this, every category loses money
- **Central region has -10.4% profit margin** despite ₹501k in sales — highest discount rate (24%) is the culprit
- **Tables lost ₹17,725** — most loss-making sub-category, avg discount 26%
- **Machines look profitable overall (+₹3,384) but 3 out of 4 regions are already in loss** — East is masking the reality
